Game Info
  • There are 10 questions to answer.
  • You have 15 seconds to answer each question.
  • At 3 seconds the screen will shake to remind you to select an answer.
  • If the time runs out before answering, it will be classed as a wrong answer and the next question will be displayed.
  • Try to answer questions as quickly as possible.
  • Your score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.
  • Good Luck!
